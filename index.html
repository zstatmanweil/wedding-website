Our Wedding

After eight years of making each other laugh and grow, we have decided to get married! It was hard to imagine what a wedding would look like for us, especially after the last two unpredictable years keeping social gatherings up in the air. After much thought, we have decided to have a small weekend gathering in June with our closest family and a couple friends. Family is very important to both of us and we are excited to have a small event so we can spend quality time with each of you and have our families finally meet!

Our dear future neighbors have graciously offered to let us use their house in Healdsburg right on the Russian River as the main location for the celebration. As the area where the Statman-Weils vacationed as kids, and as a spot we visit regularly for birthdays and long weekends, it is very meaningful to us that we will be getting married along the Russian River, and at this house in particular. We can't wait to share it with you all!
